Concerts with a Cause

Project Type

Trifold Brochure and Logo Design

Role

Designer

Client

Bryn Mawr Presbyterian Church, Music and Fine Arts Department

Tools Used

Photoshop
Illustrator
InDesign

Year

2019

Project Details

In 2019, BMPC's Music and Fine Arts Department, created the Concerts with a Cause music series. Throughout 2019 into 2020, they hosted numerous amounts of concerts up until the COVID-19 pandemic hit. Starting with the logo I created, this expanded into a trifold brochure for the department and eventually a banner that was displayed out front of the church.
